What we do

Creative-wall is a manufacture company of decorative products for interior environment. We take advantage of latest technology and our high grade gypsum quality to design and produce healthy, durable and fire-safe decorative materials. Our finest decoration panel consist of fiber reinforced selected gypsum with special additives for additional strength and lightweight fillers.



3D Stones

Cladding stones are designed in smaller scales providing a unique pattern of stony structure. You can unfold your inspiration even in tighter areas where panels might be too large to be applied.

3D Wall Panels

True concept of dimensional wall derives from 3D panels that make possible creation of continuous sculptural wall. They are designed to be strongly joined, stay stable and provide a perfect alignment, thus creating uninterrupted artistic view on your surroundings.
